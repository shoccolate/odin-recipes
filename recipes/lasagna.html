Lasagna Recipe

Just a basic classic lasagna recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles (250g)
  • Ground beef (500g)
  • Tomato sauce (400g)
  • Mozzarella cheese (200g)

Instructions:

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
